## **The 7 Dimensions Explained**

### **1. ğŸ› Spiritual (Sacraments & Prayer)**

**What it tracks:**
- Baptisms (families, dates, godparents, follow-up formation)
- First Communions (catechesis attendance, readiness assessment)
- Confirmations (sponsor assignments, retreat attendance)
- Marriages (premarital counseling completion, anniversary dates)
- Holy Orders & Religious Profession (vocational discernment tracking)
- Funerals (family support, grief group enrollment)
- Prayer circles (intention requests, outcomes, community bonding)
- Reconciliation (frequency, themes for spiritual direction)

**Why it matters:** The Church's highest calling is **sacramental life**. Tracking ensures:
- No one misses sacraments they're ready for
- Pastoral follow-up after key moments
- Families feel personally known
- Prayer requests receive communal support

### **2. ğŸ Material (Food, Shelter, Healthcare, Education)**

**What it tracks:**
- Food pantry (beneficiaries, dietary needs, frequency, donated items)
- Homeless shelter (beds available, residents, case management)
- Medical clinic (services offered, patients served, volunteer doctors)
- Tutoring program (students, subjects, progress tracking, success rates)
- Emergency assistance (rent, utilities, medical bills covered)
- Daycare (enrollment, subsidized spots, safety compliance)

**Why it matters:** **"Whatever you do to the least of these..."** (Matthew 25). Tracking ensures:
- Resources reach those most in need (not based on connections)
- No one falls through cracks
- Dignity preserved (personalized approach, not charity-as-transaction)
- Impact measured (show donors what their gifts accomplish)

### **3. ğŸ‘¥ Community (Pastoral Care & Relationship)**

**What it tracks:**
- Homebound visits (Who? When? What was shared? Follow-up needed?)
- Grief support (Death, job loss, divorceâ€”who needs sustained care?)
- Conflict resolution (Mediation offered; relationships restored)
- Marriage prep (Couples tracked; success rate measured)
- Baptismal sponsor formation (Teaching godparent role)
- New parishioner integration (Welcome, connection, small group assignment)
- Youth mentorship (Teens paired with adults; trust-building milestones)

**Why it matters:** The Church is a **body**, not a building. Tracking ensures:
- People aren't isolated
- Crises are noticed + addressed
- New members feel wanted
- Trust is actively built

### **4. âš–ï¸ Justice (Advocacy & Community Organizing)**

**What it tracks:**
- Active campaigns (Living wage, refugee rights, environment, housing)
- Participation (Who's involved? Letters written? Actions taken?)
- Impact (Did the company raise wages? Did the law change? How many benefited?)
- Coalition partners (Other churches, NGOs, secular groups working together)
- Educational events (Learn-ins, talks, screenings about justice)
- Voting blocs (Coordinate Catholic voters for justice-aligned candidates)

**Why it matters:** **"Seek justice, love mercy, walk humbly..."** (Micah 6:8). Tracking ensures:
- Justice isn't vague ("we care about poor people")â€”it's **concrete** (this campaign won 2,500 workers a raise)
- Parishes amplify power through coordination
- Faith + action are linked
- Impact is measured + celebrated

### **5. ğŸ‘¦ Formation (Education & Spiritual Growth)**

**What it tracks:**

- RCIA (Inquirers, catechumens, candidatesâ€”pace of formation tracked)
- Youth groups (Middle school, high school, young adultsâ€”activities, attendance, leadership)
- Adult Bible study (Groups, topics, engagement levels)
- Marriages ministry (Sponsor couples, enrichment events, divorce recovery)
- Senior formation (Spiritual direction, legacy projects, mentoring youth)

**Why it matters:** **"Teach them to observe all that I commanded..."** (Matthew 28:20). Tracking ensures:
- Formation is intentional (not ad hoc)
- People progress at right pace (not rushed, not stalled)
- Mentorship is structured (pairing, accountability)
- Generational knowledge transfers (elders mentoring youth)

### **6. ğŸ’° Stewardship (Giving & Transparency)**

- Individual giving (Donations, frequency, interestsâ€”e.g., "wants to fund justice work")

**Why it matters:** **Money is a spiritual issue.** Tracking ensures:
- Trust (Transparency eliminates accusations of misuse)
- Alignment (Giving supports stated priorities)
- Generosity (When people see impact, they give more)
- Sustainability (Can we fund this program long-term?)

### **7. ğŸ“‹ Admin (Coordination & Communication)**

**Why it matters:** **"Let all things be done decently and in order..."** (1 Cor. 14:40). Tracking ensures:
- Smooth operations (no double-bookings, missed Masses)
- Safety (background checks done, allergies noted, emergency plan updated)
